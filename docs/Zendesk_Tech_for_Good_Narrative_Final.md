## Project Title

OUR CLEAR HAVEN: A Digital Support Platform Connecting Oregonians Experiencing Homelessness to Shelter, Resources, and Hope

---

## Executive Summary

Founded by George Scharfetter after experiencing homelessness in Bend, Oregon, OUR CLEAR HAVEN was built to solve the exact problems he faced navigating a fragmented system. Guided by the motto "Clear steps. Real hope.", it is a mobile and web platform designed specifically for individuals experiencing homelessness and housing insecurity in Oregon. We combine practical resource navigation, trauma-informed design, and responsive support to reduce barriers and help people move toward stability.

---

## The Problem

On any given night, over 20,000 Oregonians experience homelessness, with more than 580,000 at risk of housing loss. Existing systems are fragmented and difficult to navigate during moments of crisis. Many people find resources inconsistent, hard to access, or not designed for urgent use — resulting in prolonged homelessness, repeated crises, and 1 in 3 unable to connect with needed services.

---

## Our Solution

OUR CLEAR HAVEN provides a single, stigma-free digital entry point where users can quickly find shelter, food, transportation, healthcare, legal aid, and crisis support. The platform emphasizes dignity, clarity, and reliable follow-through.

---

## High-Value Zendesk Integration Opportunities

Zendesk will serve as the reliable operational backbone, while our custom AI support layer provides a warm, empathetic user experience. This separation allows us to combine human-centered guidance with logistical reliability and consistent follow-through.

Specific uses include:

- Support ticketing and AI-assisted triage for common needs (shelter, food, transportation, legal aid).
- Self-service Help Center as a searchable public resource directory.
- Live messaging and chat for accessible, conversational support.
- Crisis escalation workflows with warm handoffs to 988 Lifeline when appropriate.
- Donor, volunteer, and sponsor coordination tools.

---

## Trauma-Informed and Technical Approach

We will implement privacy-conscious workflows and structured data handling to protect sensitive user information. Zendesk will be seamlessly integrated with our Firebase-powered mobile interface via APIs. Sunshine Custom Objects will help organize service interactions while generating anonymized insights into service gaps for policymakers.

---

## Impact and Reach

Initial launch will focus on Central Oregon with a goal of serving 1,000+ unique users in the first 12 months. Success will be measured by increased service connections, reduced friction in accessing help, improved user experience, and a reduction in repeat crisis contacts as users connect to stable services.

---

## Why Zendesk Tech for Good?

Zendesk's professional-grade tools — Help Center, Messaging, AI Agents, and reporting — will give a small mission-driven team the infrastructure needed to operate reliably and scale responsibly. This partnership aligns directly with our goal of delivering clear steps and real hope to vulnerable Oregonians.
