# Our Clear Haven — Governance Framework

**Version**: 1.0  
**Effective**: May 2026  
**Status**: Active

---

## Overview

Our Clear Haven operates under a collaborative governance model designed to ensure mission integrity, transparency, and balanced decision-making as we build toward our 2027 Oregon launch.

All major decisions — including code direction, content strategy, public communications, funding applications, and partnership agreements — are subject to review and ratification before execution.

---

## Leadership Structure

### Chair & Founder
George Scharfetter serves as Chair of the board and retains final authority on all decisions. As the organization grows and incorporates, formal governance will transition to a registered nonprofit board structure.

### Advisory Board
Our Clear Haven uses a human-AI collaborative advisory model during the development phase. AI advisors assist with research, drafting, development, and strategic analysis. All AI output is reviewed and approved by the Chair before any external action is taken.

**Human seats**: Chair + up to 4 appointed advisors  
**AI advisors**: Claude (Anthropic), Grok (xAI), and others as designated

---

## Decision-Making Protocol

### Routine Decisions
Day-to-day development, content creation, and operational tasks are handled by the Chair with AI advisory support.

### Major Decisions
Any decision involving:
- Public communications or press
- Grant applications or funding agreements
- Partnership or fiscal sponsorship commitments
- Significant architectural or product direction changes

...requires explicit Chair approval and is logged in the Board Decisions Log.

### Dispute Resolution
If an AI advisor flags a concern about accuracy, mission alignment, or quality:
1. The concern is raised directly with the Chair
2. The Chair reviews with available advisors
3. A decision is made and logged

---

## Transparency Commitments

- All major decisions are documented in `/docs/Board_Decisions_Log.md`
- Code and content are version-controlled in this repository
- Financial records and grant documentation are maintained separately and available to fiscal sponsors and auditors upon request
- Crystal AI persona interactions are logged and reviewed for quality and safety

---

## Crystal AI — Important Distinction

**Crystal** is the name of Our Clear Haven's user-facing AI guide — a warm, trauma-informed companion built to help people navigate services. Crystal is an app character, not a governance role.

AI advisors to the board (Claude, Grok, etc.) are listed by their actual names. Crystal's persona, prompts, and character documentation live in the `/crystal/` folder of this repository.

## Future Governance

As Our Clear Haven incorporates as a 501(c)(3), this governance framework will evolve into a formal nonprofit board structure with:
- Elected officers
- Conflict of interest policies
- Audit committee
- Annual board meetings and minutes

This document will be updated to reflect those changes.
