# Our Clear Haven

**Clear steps. Real hope.**

A free mobile and web platform connecting Oregonians experiencing homelessness and housing instability to shelter, food, healthcare, legal aid, and community — around the clock, in plain language, with no barriers to entry.

**Website**: [ourclearhaven.org](https://ourclearhaven.org)  
**Support the mission**: [gofundme.com/f/build-our-clear-haven-ai-for-homeless-help](https://gofundme.com/f/build-our-clear-haven-ai-for-homeless-help)  
**Oregon launch**: 2027

---

## The Problem

On any given night in Oregon:

- **20,000+** people experience homelessness
- **580,000+** are at risk of housing instability
- **1 in 3** cannot access services when they need them most
- **0** unified, trauma-informed statewide platforms exist to help them navigate the system

## What We're Building

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Resource Directory** | Curated local services with real-time availability, launching in Central Oregon |
| **Crystal AI Companion** | Empathetic, trauma-informed guidance via voice and text — available 24/7 |
| **Peer Support Network** | AI-moderated community with built-in safety tools and crisis routing to 988 |
| **Secure Document Vault** | Encrypted storage for IDs, medical records, and critical benefit documents |
| **Step-by-Step Navigation** | Plain-language pathways through housing, legal, health, and benefit systems |

## Who We Serve

- Individuals and families experiencing homelessness or acute housing instability
- People navigating reentry, domestic violence, or mental health emergencies
- Low-tech users — designed for those without consistent device or internet access
- Advocates and case managers coordinating care

## About the Founder

Our Clear Haven was founded by George Scharfetter after personally experiencing homelessness in Bend, Oregon. The platform was built from the ground up to solve the exact problems he encountered navigating the system — unclear information, fragmented services, and no single place to turn.

Crystal, the app's AI guide, was named after a kind motel manager who helped George when he needed it most.
